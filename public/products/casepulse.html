Questions

Good to know

Who is CasePulse for?

Indian advocates, chambers and law firms who want to run the whole practice — cases, cause lists, clients, drafting, time and billing — from one web platform instead of scattered tools.

How is CasePulse different from a case-lookup app?

Lookup tools just show a status. CasePulse pulls that data in automatically and adds everything around it: cause lists, a display board, a client portal, drafting, calendar, documents, time tracking, billing and reports.

Do I have to enter case data by hand?

No. Add a case by its CNR and CasePulse fetches the details from eCourts, then keeps status and next dates current in the background.

Can my whole team use it?

Yes. CasePulse is built around a shared workspace with multiple users, roles and permissions, so your associates and staff work from the same live data.

Does it work on mobile?

CasePulse runs in any modern browser, so it works on your laptop and phone. For a lighter, phone-first case tracker, our sibling product LegalTrack may suit solo advocates.

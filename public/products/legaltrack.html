Questions

Good to know

Is LegalTrack only for Indian advocates?

Yes. It's built specifically for India-first legal workflows — eCourts case data, Indian court hierarchies, and the way advocates here manage matters.

Does it only track cases?

No. Case status is just the starting point. LegalTrack manages your documents, hearing and case notes, reminders, client notifications and payment tracking around each matter.

How is it different from eCourts or case-lookup apps?

Those show you status. LegalTrack pulls that same status in automatically, then helps you manage the entire matter — so you stop switching between apps, files, notes and reminders.

Do you support UPI payments from clients?

Not yet. You can record dues and receipts manually per case and send clients a payment reminder. Direct UPI collection can be added later.
